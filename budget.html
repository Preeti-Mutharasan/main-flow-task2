Budgeting Tips for Traveling

Back Home

1. Plan Your Budget

Estimate your total expenses, including transportation, accommodation, food, and activities. Always allocate extra funds for emergencies.

2. Book in Advance

Save money by booking flights, accommodations, and activities early. Look out for discounts and deals.

3. Use Public Transportation

Reduce transportation costs by using buses, trains, or metro systems instead of taxis or rideshares.

4. Choose Affordable Dining Options

Eat at local restaurants or cook your meals if possible. Avoid touristy spots to save money.

5. Track Your Expenses

Keep track of your spending using a notebook or budgeting app to avoid overspending.

Approximate Travel Budgets

Asia: ₹2,500–₹5,000 per day (budget-friendly options available in Southeast Asia).

Africa: ₹4,000–₹8,000 per day (varies depending on the country and activities).

Europe: ₹6,000–₹12,000 per day (Western Europe is more expensive than Eastern Europe).
