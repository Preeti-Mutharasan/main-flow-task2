Mount Kilimanjaro

Mount Kilimanjaro, located in Tanzania, is the tallest mountain in Africa at 5,895 meters. Known as the "Roof of Africa," it is a dormant volcano with three cones and attracts climbers worldwide for its breathtaking views and rich biodiversity.

Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town, located in South Africa, is a vibrant coastal city known for its stunning Table Mountain, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural heritage. It is a popular tourist destination, offering landmarks like Robben Island and the Cape of Good Hope.

Marrakech, Morocco

Marrakech, a historic city in Morocco, is famed for its vibrant souks, stunning palaces, and the bustling Jemaa el-Fnaa square. It offers a rich blend of culture, history, and architecture, surrounded by the beauty of the Atlas Mountains.
