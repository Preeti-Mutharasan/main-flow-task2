Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light" and is famed for its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. It is a global hub for art, fashion, and romance.

Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, is known as the "Eternal City" and boasts iconic landmarks like the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Vatican City. It is a treasure trove of history, art, and culture.

London

London, the capital of England, is a vibrant city known for iconic landmarks like Big Ben, the Tower of London, and Buckingham Palace. It is a global hub for history, culture, and entertainment.
